## The Pioneers of Permaculture

**David Holmgren and Bill Mollison** have been pivotal in developing the principles of permaculture. Their insights into sustainable living and ecological stewardship continue to inspire individuals and communities worldwide.

**For more information on Bill Mollison's contributions to permaculture:**  
Visit the [Permaculture Research Institute](https://www.permaculturenews.org/)

**For more information about David Holmgren's work:**  
Visit his official website [Holmgren Permaculture Design](https://holmgren.com.au/)

## From Permaculture to Living Systems

**Rob Hopkins**, the co-founder of the Transition Movement, has been inspirational in translating permaculture principles to actionable insights to help people come together to reimagine the future and solve society's big crises. 

Check out his personal website [Rob Hopkins' Blog](https://www.robhopkins.net/) and [the Transition Network](https://transitionnetwork.org/).
