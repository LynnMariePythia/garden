# The Edge of Chaos
## Where Innovation Emerges from Uncertainty

*A message from Lynn Marie DePippo*

Learn why I find such inspiration, creativity and beauty from entrepreneurs at the edge of chaos and how this relates to Living Systems Investing and our work at Pythia Capital.

Developing the LSI (Living Systems Investing) framework has been my passion project for a long time - to help both investors and entrepreneurs embrace unconventional thinking and navigate chaos towards creative, systemic solutions to complex problems.

**The edge of chaos is where all the magic happens.**

---

## Harnessing the Energy of Uncertainty

When maverick entrepreneurs and investors hang around the edge of chaos, it sparks creativity, inspiration, and even joy. 

Yes, there is uncertainty and fear of the unknown. But if we can harness that energy, it can lead to breakthroughs. 

We need that kind of creative thinking now more than ever with all the global crises we face. Linear thinking and centralized control have led us to this point. We need more decentralized, diverse solutions that allow local empowerment. 

Nature shows us how complex adaptive systems self-organize in wise ways.

---

## The Problem with Following the Herd

Too often investors pursue trendy markets, leading to herd behavior and unintended consequences. We leave behind the real fringe visionaries, who see the world differently. 

When they come together, their ideas can be world changing.

**Fringe entrepreneurs are generally polymath entrepreneurs.** These individuals have skills and expertise across multiple diverse disciplines and they often apply this broad knowledge to entrepreneurial pursuits. Their cross-disciplinary thinking gives them an edge in discovering new breakthrough opportunities.

The current inflexible investment strategies and structures do not align with the requirements of these exceptional entrepreneurs. Our focus lies on the investment structure and strategy rather than considering systemic value, resulting in bubbles and a zero-sum game scenario. 

These interdisciplinary entrepreneurs possess a distinct perspective, recognizing both challenges and prospects uniquely. They demonstrate an ability to swiftly pinpoint groundbreaking innovations and effortlessly adapt when necessary - a crucial skill in navigating today's fast-paced technological and scientific advancements.

---

## Supporting the Overlooked Visionaries

I created Pythia Capital to support those unconventional entrepreneurs who are often overlooked. 

Our approach is based on the principles of living systems, not outdated economic models that do not reflect the reality of nested, complex systems. 

That helps us to see opportunities others miss, and fund solutions that are truly systemic, impactful, elegant and beautiful.

There is no window dressing or narrative shaping possible following natural wisdom. We shine a torch of light into the dark crevices. 

However, it is the light shining in the darkness that illuminates new inspiration, shares wisdom to make sure you learn from past mistakes, and helps you find better balance with your plans. 

**The time is perfect for new approaches as the old paradigms crumble.**

---

## Finding Our Way Through Chaos

I believe if we look to nature's intelligence and empower creative thinkers, we can find our way through the chaos to a more balanced and vibrant world.

The edge of chaos isn't a place to fear. It's a place to explore. It's where the old breaks down and the new emerges. It's where the most creative solutions to our most complex problems are waiting to be discovered.
