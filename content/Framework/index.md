# Framework
## The Living Systems Investing Methodology

Living Systems Investing (LSI) is a methodology that applies permaculture principles, complexity science, and ancient wisdom to business development and capital allocation.

It's not about ESG compliance or impact investing buzzwords. It's about understanding how living systems actually work - and using those principles to identify breakthrough innovation, manage risk, and create regenerative value.

## Why This Framework Works

These principles aren't arbitrary rules. They're based on billions of years of evolution - nature's tested methods for creating resilient, adaptive, sustainable systems.

When we apply them to investing:
- We see opportunities others miss
- We manage risk better  
- We identify innovation earlier
- We create aligned incentives

**Nature doesn't do quarterly earnings.** Nature does sustainability, resilience, and emergent value creation.
