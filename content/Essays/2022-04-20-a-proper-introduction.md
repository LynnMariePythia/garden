My name is Lynn Marie DePippo, and I am the Founder and CEO of Pythia Capital. I started out in investment research and portfolio management many years ago. I was fortunate to focus in areas with many brilliant, entrepreneurial people applying science and technology to add positive value in the world. I worked with a variety of different styles of investing over the years, but in many ways, I felt like "The Little Princess" always looking for my true home. One day I took a shaman class at the Omega Institute and a permaculture class at University of Massachusetts. I grew up in the Northeast and was always very brainy and analytical, so this strange path caught me off guard, but over time, filled me with wonder and awe. There were very challenging times and much soul searching along the way. I discovered that both ancient mysticism and permaculture both have strong links with newly emerging science, technology, and mathematics such as complexity science, chaos theory, quantum science, geometry and even blockchain. These realizations have changed my life in profound ways.
