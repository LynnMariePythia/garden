It is good to see efforts in the cannabis industry working to make sure there are quality companies and professional procedures.   I like to see businesses like this. This is the only way we will be able to sustain healthy growth in this field with such a long and troubled history. There are few industries that can provide such a wealth of opportunities for both small businesses as well as large businesses. It is easy to always be enamored by the next billion dollar start up but we can not forget that the vibrancy of our economy depends on both local and global businesses. There are always ways to make money by forward thinking and flexible investors that are looking to make money by contributing to the health and well being of the people, economy and the environment. Sometimes it requires working outside of the most common existing investment paradigms and structures and collaborating with other entrepreneurs and investors that are thinking along the same lines.
